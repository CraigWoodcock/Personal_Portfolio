About Me

Who Am I?

I’m a father of 2 girls from the West Midlands – more specifically a small town called tipton – 5 minutes walk from the Black Country Museum where the boat yard scenes were filmed for the tv show 'Peaky Blinders'.

When I Left school I went to college to study electrical installation but dropped out after the first year as I realised it wasn’t for me and I was more suited to customer service/retail roles.

I Worked at Asda where I got promoted to service section leader fairly quickly and then went on to hold several supervisory/duty management roles. I've been a fork lift driver working in cold storage and I've even worked at mcdonalds.

I find that the diversity of roles that I've held gives me the experience and ability to see perspectives from all departments – see how processes work, spot in-efficiencies and to help make continuous improvements to the process
